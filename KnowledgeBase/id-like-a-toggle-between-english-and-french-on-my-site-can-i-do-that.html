id like a toggle between english and french on my site - can i Do that

Created: 2026-06-16T15:08:29.172868+00:00
Question: id like a toggle between english and french on my site - can i Do that?

Answer

Non-technical explanation

Yes, you can toggle between English and French on your site using Recite Me’s toolbar. There are two main ways to achieve this:

  1. Language dropdown: Add a dropdown menu to your site that lets users select between English and French.
  2. Custom toggle button: Use a button on your site to cycle through languages (e.g., click to switch between English and French).

Both methods require configuring the Recite Me toolbar with your client ID and specifying the desired languages in the settings.

---

Technical explanation

To enable language toggling, use the following configurations in your website’s HTML:

#### 1. Language dropdown (standalone selector) Add this script snippet to your site, replacing YOUR_CLIENT_ID with your actual client key:

<script
  src="https://cdn.reciteme.com/reciteme_toolbar_launcher.min.js"
  data-clientid="YOUR_CLIENT_ID"
  data-buttons="language-dropdown"
  data-button-language-dropdown-languages='[
    {"code":"en","name":"English"},
    {"code":"fr","name":"French"}
  ]'
  data-button-language-dropdown-show-native-name="true"
>
</script>

This creates a dropdown menu where users can select between English and French [2].

#### 2. Custom toggle button (translate cycle) Add a button to your HTML and link it to the toolbar:

<button id="language-toggle">Language</button>

<script
  src="https://cdn.reciteme.com/reciteme_toolbar_launcher.min.js"
  data-clientid="YOUR_CLIENT_ID"
  data-buttons="none"
  data-translate-cycle-attach-to="#language-toggle"
  data-button-translate-cycle-language-codes="en,fr"
  data-button-translate-cycle-language-names="English,French"
>
</script>

This attaches the language toggle to your button, allowing users to switch between English and French with a click [5].

---

Recommended next steps

  1. Verify client ID: Ensure your YOUR_CLIENT_ID is correctly configured in the Recite Me admin panel [3].
  2. Test language options: Confirm the languages are supported by your Recite Me plan and client settings.
  3. Customize UI: Adjust dropdown/button styling or labels as needed for your site’s design.

If you encounter issues, check the [Recite Me support documentation](https://docs.reciteme.com/) for troubleshooting.
