What CSP allowlist is required for Recite Me launcher, toolbar, and events

Created: 2026-06-16T15:14:48.085605+00:00
Question: What CSP allowlist is required for Recite Me launcher, toolbar, and events?

Answer

Non-technical explanation

If your site uses a Content Security Policy (CSP), Recite Me will only work when Recite Me domains are explicitly allowed. At minimum, clients should whitelist *.reciteme.com so the launcher script, toolbar services, and events reporting can load and run.

Technical explanation

For CSP-restricted sites, add Recite Me domains for script loading, API/service calls, and telemetry.

Required Recite Me endpoints:

  • Launcher script: cdn.reciteme.com
  • Toolbar/API (region-specific): api.reciteme.com, uswestapi.reciteme.com, auapi.reciteme.com
  • Events/statistics: events.reciteme.com

Recommended baseline policy fragment:

Content-Security-Policy:
  script-src 'self' https://cdn.reciteme.com https://*.reciteme.com;
  connect-src 'self' https://api.reciteme.com https://uswestapi.reciteme.com https://auapi.reciteme.com https://events.reciteme.com https://*.reciteme.com;

If your CSP is strict and explicit, list each hostname directly. If your policy allows controlled wildcards, https://*.reciteme.com can simplify rollout across regions and services.

Recommended next steps

  1. Add the Recite Me hostnames to your CSP in your primary environment first (staging/UAT).
  2. Validate launcher load from cdn.reciteme.com.
  3. Confirm toolbar requests succeed against your assigned regional API hostname.
  4. Confirm event calls to events.reciteme.com are not blocked.
  5. Repeat validation in production after deployment.

What to verify

  • Browser console/network shows no CSP violations for reciteme.com domains.
  • Launcher script request returns 200 from cdn.reciteme.com.
  • Toolbar API requests succeed for your region endpoint.
  • Events requests to events.reciteme.com are allowed (not blocked by CSP).
